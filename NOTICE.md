# Exercise illustrations

The compact exercise illustrations in `app/src/main/res/drawable-nodpi/` are
downscaled, unmodified previews of artwork by **Everkinetic**, made available
through Wikimedia Commons under the [Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0
Unported license](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/).

Source collection: [Wikimedia Commons: Weight training diagrams](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Weight_training_diagrams)

The included files depict lat pulldown, pull-up, incline chest press, shoulder
press, seated row, pec/reverse fly, biceps curl, triceps press, leg press,
leg extension/curl, hip machine, calf raise, back extension, crunch, dumbbell
bench press, squat, Romanian deadlift, plank, push-up, and walking motion.
